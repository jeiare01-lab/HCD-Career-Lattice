# 📖 User Guide — HCD Career Lattice v1.1

**Complete step-by-step instructions for every feature.**

---

## 🎯 **SECTION 1: GETTING STARTED**

### **Step 1: Access the App**

1. Open web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
2. Go to: `https://hcd-career-lattice.vercel.app`
3. App loads automatically
4. You see two tabs at top:
   - 📊 Grade Lattice
   - 🛤️ Build My Path

✅ **You're ready to use the app!**

---

### **Step 2: Understand the Main Views**

#### **View 1: Grade Lattice (Browse)**
- Rows: All HCD sections (Training & Assessment, HR, Finance, etc.)
- Columns: Job grades (JG1 through JG15)
- Cells: Contain role names (colored by level)
- Purpose: **Explore all available positions**

#### **View 2: Build My Path (Plan)**
- Shows roles you've selected
- Displays transitions between roles
- Shows competency gaps
- Shows training needed
- Purpose: **Create your career progression**

---

## 📊 **SECTION 2: BROWSING THE GRADE LATTICE**

### **Step 1: Navigate the Grid**

1. Click **"📊 Grade Lattice"** tab
2. You see a large grid with:
   - Sections listed on left (rows)
   - Grades JG1-JG15 across top (columns)
   - Role names in cells

3. **To scroll right:**
   - Use horizontal scroll bar at bottom
   - Or swipe left on mobile/tablet

4. **To scroll down:**
   - Use vertical scroll bar on right
   - Or swipe down on mobile/tablet

✅ **Tip:** Color changes show career level progression left-to-right

---

### **Step 2: Find a Specific Role**

#### **By Section (Row)**
1. Find the section name on the left
2. Scroll down if needed to locate it
3. Row shows all positions in that section

#### **By Grade (Column)**
1. Find the grade at top (JG1-JG15)
2. Scroll right if needed
3. Column shows all positions at that grade

#### **Example: Find "Trainer II"**
1. Find "Training & Assessment" row (TVET section)
2. Find "JG5" column (Intermediate grade)
3. Click the "Trainer II" button in that cell

✅ **Result:** Detail panel opens on right side

---

### **Step 3: View Role Details**

When you click a role, a detail panel appears showing:


### **Understanding the Detail Panel**

| Section | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| Function & Section | Where this role sits organizationally |
| Title | Job position name |
| Grade | JG1-JG15 classification |
| Level | Entry/Intermediate/Officer/Supervisory/Managerial |
| Education | Required degree or equivalent |
| Experience | Years needed before this role |
| Competencies | Skills needed with proficiency level |
| Training | Program recommended at this level |

✅ **Action:** Click "Add to My Path" to include in your progression

## 🛤️ **SECTION 3: BUILDING YOUR CAREER PATH**

### **Step 1: Add Your First Role**

1. Browse Grade Lattice
2. Click any role you're interested in
3. Detail panel opens
4. Click **"+ Add to My Path"** button
5. Button text changes to **"Added to Path"** (grayed out)

✅ **Result:** Role added to your custom path

---

### **Step 2: Add More Roles**

1. Go back to Grade Lattice
2. Click another role (ideally a higher grade)
3. Click **"+ Add to My Path"**
4. Repeat for 3-5 roles total

📌 **Tip:** Try to go up in grades (JG3 → JG5 → JG8) to see progression

---

### **Step 3: View Your Path**

1. Click **"🛤️ Build My Path"** tab
2. You see all roles you added
3. Roles displayed in order added

```
Step #1: Role Name (Grade/Level)
         [Remove Button]

       ↓ [Transition Details]

Step #2: Role Name (Grade/Level)
         [Remove Button]

       ↓ [More transitions]

Step #3: Role Name (Grade/Level)
```

---

### **Step 4: Understand Transition Details**

Between each pair of roles, you see what's needed to move forward:

```
↑ Moving from TAss III to Trainer I:

Core Competencies to develop:
• Planning and Organizing: Basic → Intermediate
• Leadership: Basic → Intermediate

Technical Competencies to develop:
• Project/Prog Management: None → Moderately required

Additional experience: 1-2 years needed
```

**Read as:** "To move from TAss III to Trainer I, you need to develop these skills and gain this experience"

---

### **Step 5: Remove or Reorganize**

#### **To Remove a Role:**
1. Find the role in your path
2. Click **[Remove]** button on that step
3. Role disappears from path
4. All transitions recalculate automatically

#### **To Reorganize:**
1. Remove roles that are out of order
2. Re-add them in correct sequence
3. Or start fresh with new path

---

## 📚 **SECTION 4: UNDERSTANDING TRAINING ROADMAP**

### **Step 1: View Training in Role Detail**

1. Click any role
2. Scroll down detail panel
3. See **"📚 Recommended Training"** section
4. Shows:
   - Program name
   - Cost (₱800 - ₱3,500)
   - Target audience (SPECIALISTS, GENERALISTS, etc.)
   - Sample modules (first 3 + count of rest)

**Example:**
```
Functional Training Programs
Cost: ₱1,500.00 • Priority: GENERALISTS

Key Modules:
• HR Management 101
• Site Management & Administration 101  
• Labor Relations 101
+ 5 more modules
```

### **Step 2: View Full Training Roadmap**

1. Go to **"🛤️ Build My Path"** tab
2. Build a path (add 2-3 roles)
3. Scroll down to **"📚 Training Roadmap for Your Path"**
4. See all training programs needed

**Shows for each level in your path:**
- Program name
- Cost
- Target audience
- Sample modules

```
Entry-Level: Program On-the-GO
💰 Cost: ₱800.00 • SPECIALISTS
Key Modules: Work Attitude & Values • 360° Feedback + 6 more

Intermediate: Functional Training Programs
💰 Cost: ₱1,500.00 • GENERALISTS
Key Modules: HR Management 101 • Site Management 101 + 6 more

Officer: Leadership Foundations
💰 Cost: ₱1,800.00 • LEADS
Key Modules: Basic Supervision • Developing Leaders + 9 more
```

### **Step 3: Understand Training Programs**

| Level | Program | Cost | Audience | Modules |
|-------|---------|------|----------|---------|
| Entry (JG1-3) | Program On-the-GO | ₱800 | SPECIALISTS | 8 |
| Intermediate (JG4-6) | Functional Training | ₱1,500 | GENERALISTS | 8 |
| Officer (JG7-9) | Leadership Foundations | ₱1,800 | LEADS | 11 |
| Supervisory (JG10-12) | Leadership Imperatives | ₱2,500 | BPs | 7 |
| Managerial (JG13-15) | Executive Leadership | ₱3,500 | SPs | 11 |

**What this means:** At each career level, you'll complete the training for that level.

### **Step 4: Calculate L&D Investment**

Example 3-step path:
```
TAss III (JG3 Entry)
  ↓ Needs: Program On-the-GO = ₱800

Trainer I (JG4 Intermediate)  
  ↓ Needs: Functional Training = ₱1,500

Lead Trainer II (JG8 Officer)
  ↓ Needs: Leadership Foundations = ₱1,800

Total L&D Investment: ₱4,100
```

## 🖨️ **SECTION 5: EXPORTING & SHARING**

### **Feature 1: Print Path**

**What it does:** Creates a printable PDF of your path

**How to use:**
1. Build a path (add at least 1 role)
2. Click **"🖨️ Print Path"** button
3. Browser print dialog opens
4. Click **"Print"** or **"Save as PDF"**

**What you get:**
- Your complete path with all details
- Formatted professionally
- PGB branding
- Date generated
- IDP reminder note

**File name:** `Career-Path-[date-time].pdf`

---

### **Feature 2: Save as JSON**

**What it does:** Downloads structured data file

**How to use:**
1. Build a path
2. Click **"💾 Save (JSON)"** button
3. File downloads automatically

**What you get:**
- Structured data format
- All path details in JSON
- Can be imported elsewhere

**File name:** `Career-Path-[date-time].json`

---

### **Feature 3: Export as CSV**

**What it does:** Downloads spreadsheet-compatible file

**How to use:**
1. Build a path
2. Click **"📊 Export (CSV)"** button
3. File downloads automatically

**What you get:**
- Comma-separated values
- Open in Excel/Google Sheets
- One row per role in path

**File name:** `Career-Path-[date-time].csv`

**Columns:**
- Step
- Position Title
- Grade
- Level
- Function
- Section
- Experience Required
- Education Required

---

### **Feature 4: Send to Supervisor**

**What it does:** Creates formal IDP discussion request

**Step 1: Start the Process**
1. Build your desired path
2. Click **"📧 Send to Supervisor"** button
3. Modal form opens with 4 fields

**Step 2: Fill Out Form**

| Field | What to Enter | Required? |
|-------|---------------|-----------|
| Supervisor's Name | Full name (e.g., "Paulette D. Liu") | Yes |
| Supervisor's Email | Work email | Yes |
| Career Aspirations & Goals | Why this path excites you, your goals | Yes |
| Preferred Discussion Date | When you want to meet | Yes |

**Example Entries:**
```
Supervisor Name: Paulette D. Liu
Email: paulette.liu@pgb.com
Aspirations: I'm passionate about developing my leadership skills 
and want to progress toward a supervisory role where I can mentor 
others and contribute to PGB's success.
Discussion Date: 2026-07-20
```

**Step 3: Submit**
1. Click **"Send & Create IDP"** button
2. Success message appears:
```
✅ Success!
Your career aspirations have been prepared. Please share this with 
your supervisor to discuss your Individual Development Plan.
```

**Step 4: Share with Supervisor**
- Email message shown in form
- Copy text and paste into email
- Or screenshot and share
- Or take to meeting

**What Supervisor Receives:**
- Formal request
- Your career aspirations
- Complete career path with all roles/grades
- Training roadmap implications
- Proposed discussion date

## 🎨 **SECTION 6: UNDERSTANDING COLORS**

### **Grade Level Colors**

```
🟢 Green (Light)    = Entry-Level (JG1-JG3)
   Specialist positions
   Example: TAss I, CADA III, AI Associate II

🔵 Blue (Light)     = Intermediate (JG4-JG6)
   Generalist positions
   Example: Trainer II, Registrar III, Teacher II

🟣 Purple (Light)   = Officer (JG7-JG9)
   Lead/senior technical roles
   Example: Lead Trainer I, Lead Teacher II, Lead Finance

🟠 Orange (Light)   = Supervisory (JG10-JG12)
   Business Partner/supervisor roles
   Example: TVET Supervisor, Section Head, Asst Supervisor

🔴 Red (Light)      = Managerial (JG13-JG15)
   Strategic Partner/Director/Manager roles
   Example: Director TVET, Manager SHS, VP level
```

### **What Colors Tell You**

- **Left side of lattice (green)** = Entry level, foundational
- **Moving right** = Career progression
- **Right side of lattice (red)** = Executive/managerial level

## ❓ **SECTION 8: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS**

### **Q: Can I save my path in the app?**
A: Paths are in-browser memory. Export as JSON to save outside app. Use Print/Export to create permanent records.

### **Q: Do I need to log in?**
A: No login needed. App works completely offline. No account required.

### **Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: Your data stays in your browser. We don't collect or store personal information. Export to save.

### **Q: Can I edit a role after adding?**
A: Not directly. Remove and re-add with correct details.

### **Q: What if a role isn't in the database?**
A: All 147+ HCD positions are included. Check different grades/sections if you can't find it.

### **Q: Can I have multiple paths?**
A: One path at a time in app. Export each path separately to keep multiple.

### **Q: What do the colored boxes mean?**
A: Colors show career level. Green=Entry, Blue=Intermediate, Purple=Officer, Orange=Supervisory, Red=Managerial.

### **Q: How accurate are the competency gaps?**
A: Gaps are calculated based on official HCD competency framework. Discuss specifics with supervisor.

### **Q: Can I print just one role?**
A: Print function prints the entire path. For one role, use the detail panel Print command from browser.

### **Q: Does this work offline?**
A: Yes, after initial load. Can view/build paths without internet.

### **Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. No login, no cloud upload, all data stays on your device.

## 🆘 **SECTION 10: TROUBLESHOOTING**

### **Problem: Can't find a role**

**Solution:**
1. Check you're looking at right section
2. Scroll right to see more grades
3. Scroll down to see more sections
4. Role might be listed under different name
5. Try searching nearby grades/sections

---

### **Problem: Button not responding**

**Solution:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh page (F5 or Cmd+R)
3. Wait a moment and try again
4. Try different browser
5. On mobile, wait for page to fully load

---

### **Problem: Export file won't download**

**Solution:**
1. Check browser's download settings
2. Allow pop-ups if prompted
3. Check Downloads folder
4. Try different file format (JSON vs CSV vs PDF)
5. Clear browser cache and retry

---

### **Problem: Print looks wrong**

**Solution:**
1. Adjust margins in print dialog
2. Select "Print background colors" option
3. Use portrait orientation
4. Try "Save as PDF" instead of printing
5. Check browser print preview first

---

### **Problem: Transition details missing**

**Solution:**
1. Need at least 2 roles in path to see transitions
2. Make sure roles are at different grades
3. Transition shows between consecutive roles
4. If path is very short, fewer transitions shown
