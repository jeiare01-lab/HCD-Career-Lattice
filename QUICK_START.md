# 🚀 Quick Start Guide — HCD Career Lattice v1.1

**Get started in 2 minutes!**

---

## 📱 **Open the App**

**Just visit:** https://hcd-career-lattice.vercel.app

No download. No installation. Works on any device!

---

## 🎯 **What You Can Do**

### **1️⃣ Browse All HCD Positions**
- Click **"📊 Grade Lattice"** tab
- See all 147+ roles organized by grade (JG1-JG15)
- Color-coded by career level

### **2️⃣ View Role Details**
- Click any role in the grid
- See:
  - Job title & grade
  - Career level (Entry, Intermediate, Officer, Supervisory, Managerial)
  - Function & section
  - Required experience & education
  - Core competencies
  - **Recommended training program**

### **3️⃣ Build Your Career Path**
- Click **"🛤️ Build My Path"** tab
- From role details, click **"+ Add to My Path"**
- Add 2-3 roles to build your progression
- See what's needed between each step:
  - Competency gaps
  - Experience needed
  - Education requirements

### **4️⃣ View Training Roadmap**
- Path automatically shows **"📚 Training Roadmap"**
- See all training programs needed for your path
- Shows cost per program (₱800-₱3,500)
- See module previews

### **5️⃣ Export or Print**
- **🖨️ Print** → Save as PDF
- **💾 Save JSON** → Keep structured data
- **📊 Export CSV** → Use in spreadsheet
- **📧 Send to Supervisor** → Start IDP discussion

## ❓ **Common Questions**

### **Q: Do I need to log in?**
**A:** No! The app works completely offline on your device.

### **Q: Where is my data saved?**
**A:** On your device only. Export to save outside the app.

### **Q: Can I print the path?**
**A:** Yes! Click "Print" and your browser's print dialog opens.

### **Q: Can I share with my supervisor?**
**A:** Yes! Click "Send to Supervisor" to prepare an email.

### **Q: What are the training programs for?**
**A:** They show what training is recommended at each career level to prepare you for advancement.

### **Q: Can I customize roles?**
**A:** This version shows fixed roles. For customization, see README.md
